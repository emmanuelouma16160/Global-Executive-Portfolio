The Global Executive Portfolio is a modern, responsive, and visually compelling executive portfolio designed to showcase professional expertise, technical skills, projects, achievements, and global impact in the technology industry.



The Global Executive Portfolio serves as a digital identity platform that highlights:

Professional background
Technical expertise
Featured projects
Certifications and achievements
Services offered
Contact and networking channels

The portfolio combines elegant design with modern development practices to create an immersive and professional experience suitable for recruiters, clients, collaborators, and tech communities worldwide. 
Languages used to build it were html and css. The portfolio is fully responsive in  all media be it mobile phone or desktop. It showcases
the owner's experience, qualification and certificatiom.
